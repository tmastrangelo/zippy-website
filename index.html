About Zippy

Zippy provides electric scooters on Elon University’s campus, allowing students to conveniently travel from class to class. With Phoenix Card integration, users can rent a scooter quickly and pay by the minute. Zippy is committed to sustainability, offering a fun and eco-friendly alternative to driving.
